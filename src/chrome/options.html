Pairing Mode (MacOS X Simulation)

Pairing mode provides MacOS X like windows management. When one of the splitted windows got focused, the other in pair will be shown together. The pair of windows always occupy the viewport without missed another one.

Remarks: It is useless in MacOS X.
  • Enable
  • Disable

Viewport - The maximum screen size

Viewport is the maximum space that your browser could use. Sometimes the detection may fail , especially for platform like Linux. In this case , you may press the "reset" button to restore the viewport to default value.

The current viewport is {{viewport}}
